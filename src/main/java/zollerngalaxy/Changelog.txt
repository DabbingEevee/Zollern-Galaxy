===== CHANGELOG =====
Zollern Galaxy v2.0.13
=====================

* Created Planet Altum, an ocean planet.
* Added blocks and items for Planet Altum.
* Zucrite Dust can now be cooked into Zucrite Ingots.
* Updated Chinese translations by EndsM.
* Added biomes for new planet.
* Fixed bug with ravines not generating correctly.
* Added extra ravine generation to Perdita.
* Added support for translations for in-game descriptions.
* Added support for underwater mobs.
* Added the Blubber Fish, Gypsy Fish and Blade Fish mobs.
    - Blade Fish will hurt you on contact.
* Added Sharks.
    - Hostile mobs, but will still spawn on Peaceful. Be careful!
    - Chance to drop shagreen (shark leather) and shark teeth.
* Added Jellyfish.
    - Basically just tiny, mean squids. They hurt and are very fast.
* Added the Abyssal Villager.
    - Model by DabbingEevee.
    - Fast Alien Villager that can breathe underwater.
    - If a GC Alien Villager spawns on Altum, it will immediately turn into an Abyssal Villager.
    - Spawns on land and in water.
    - They have their own trade items from Zollern Galaxy.
* Fixed alien squid (Squidlus) not spawning.
* Fixed Squidlus animation weirdness.
* Fixed Perdita Fueltonium Ore not pulverizing.
* Fixed Perdita and Mummy Lore Books both being Lore Book #35.
* Slightly modified texture of Zucrite block.
* Fixed hitboxes on Magmos and Frost Giant mobs.
